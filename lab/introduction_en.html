Games and Artificial Intelligence (GAI) Lab

GAI Lab is led by Chu-Hsuan Hsueh with Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST).
Starting from April 2024, we can accept Master's students.
We act together with Ikeda (Kokolo) Lab at JAIST.

If you are interested in join us, please feel free to send an email to .
In that case, please mention in the email

  1. your background (e.g., university and major),
  2. English and Japanese skills (TOEIC/TOEFL/JLPT/etc. scores if non-native),
  3. programming skills, and
  4. the research that you want to do.

Teaching Policy

  • Research topics are decided respecting students' hopes with consultation.
  • We provide students with materials on how to do research and improve presentation skills, as well as practice projects.
  • We expect students to come to lab every weekday, but there is no core time.
  • We have weekly seminars (1 presentation per month) and individual meetings.
  • We have weekday short meetings at noon (13:10-).
  • We also provide guidance as needed in addition to the periodically individual meetings.
  • The goal is to reach a level to present at least at domestic conferences.
